
# PoliScan AI 🔍⚖️
### Multi-Agent Compliance Intelligence System
 
> *"Privacy policies are written to be signed, not understood. PoliScan changes that."*
 
---
 
## What is PoliScan AI?
 
PoliScan AI is an intelligent compliance analysis system that automatically evaluates company privacy policies against real regulatory law — surfacing hidden risks, ambiguities, and compliance gaps that human reviewers often miss.
 
It doesn't just search documents. It **reasons** across them — like a legal analyst who never gets tired.
 
---
 
## The Problem
 
Privacy policies are dense, vague, and deliberately complex.  
Regulations like **GDPR** (Europe) and **DPDP** (India) are equally complex — and constantly evolving.
 
Manually comparing the two is:
- Time-consuming
- Inconsistent
- Prone to missing subtle contradictions
Most compliance tools today do keyword matching. That's not enough.
 
---
 
## The Idea
 
PoliScan uses **multiple AI agents working together** — each with a specific role in the reasoning process — to deeply analyze how a company's privacy policy aligns (or conflicts) with regulatory requirements.
 
Think of it as a panel of AI legal reviewers, each specializing in a different aspect of compliance, collaborating to produce a structured, evidence-backed report.
 
---
 
## What It Can Surface
 
- 📌 Clauses that appear compliant on the surface but contain hidden ambiguities
- 📌 Direct conflicts between company policy and regulatory law
- 📌 Missing disclosures that regulations require
- 📌 Areas of high compliance risk vs. low risk — prioritized for action
- 📌 Confidence scoring on each finding — so you know how certain the analysis is
